WHAT IS CALMQUEST?

CalmQuest is website that aims to give users the oppurtunity to understand and improve their mental health through various available resources. For example, it offers interactive surveys that reviews factors like mood or common well-being, which then gives feedback and recommendations that can improve their mental health journey. Moreover, it also offers a space for relaxtion where users can listen to different genres of music , play games, and others, which are designed to help relieve stress and find moments of calmness. Therefore, if someone wants to check in on their mental health or have moments of relaxation, CalmQuest can surely help.
