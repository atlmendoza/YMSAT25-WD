Arm Training

Build Stronger Arms

The arms consist of several key muscle groups: biceps, triceps, and forearms. To effectively train the biceps, focus on exercises like bicep curls and hammer curls, which target the front of the upper arm. Tricep dips and overhead extensions strengthen the back of the arms, while wrist curls and farmers' carries build forearm endurance and grip strength. Incorporating a variety of movements ensures balanced muscle development.

Warm-ups and stretches are essential before any arm workout to enhance flexibility and reduce the risk of injuries. Beginners can start with light weights, performing 2-3 sets of 10-12 reps, while advanced users can aim for higher intensity with progressive overload. Consistency and proper form are crucial for maximizing gains.
