Legs Training

Build Stronger Legs

Our legs are a key part of our body, you cant do anything without them! You use them for walking, jumping, climbing stairs and much more. If you need to excercise them here are the main excercises to train them:

  • Squats/weighted squats, great for your hamstrings, quadriceps and glutes
  • Lunges, effective for your quads, hamstrings and calves
  • Deadlifts, Focuses on your hamstrings, glutes and lower back
  • Leg press, Targets the hamstrings, quads and glutes
  • Calf raises, works especially for your calves
  • Leg curls, Works mainly on hamstrings
  • Leg extensions, focuses on your quadriceps
