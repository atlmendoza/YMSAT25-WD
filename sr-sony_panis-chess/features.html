History

How To Play

The rook can move and capture on a straight line. The knight can jump over and capture pieces on an L shape. The Queen can move and capture anywhere on the board but cannot jump over pieces. The king can only move one square in any direction.

Whenever the King is in check, you must the king away from the check or blocking the check with another piece. If the king is in check and there are no legal moves for it to move. The player wins through checkmate.
