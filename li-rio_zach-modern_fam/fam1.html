Dunphy Family

Dunphy Family: This is one of the main families featured on the hit television show Modern Family, which aired from 2009 to 2020. It is described as the "typical" suburban family that overcomes hardships with humor, love, and a healthy dose of dysfunction. The family consists of five members: Phil, Claire, Haley, Alex, and Luke.
