Pritchett Family

Pritchett Family: This is one of the main families featured on the hit television show Modern Family, which aired from 2009 to 2020. It showcases a multicultural, blended family dynamic that balances generational differences with humor and love. The family consists of three members: Jay, Gloria, and their son, Joe, along with Gloria’s son from a previous relationship, Manny.

Gloria Maria Pritchett

  • Gloria Maria Ramirez-Pritchett
  • Wife of Jay Pritchett
  • Mother of Manny Delgado and Joe Pritchett
  • Originally from Colombia, proud of her heritage
  • Loud, passionate, and fiercely loyal
  • Strong-willed and protective of her family
  • Overly Protective and sometimes Controlling
  • Fun-loving with a dramatic flair
  • Often misunderstood due to her accent and unique sayings
  • Has a fiery temper but a big heart
  • Catchphrase: “In my country, we have a saying…”
  • Bold and stylish, often seen in vibrant and form-fitting outfits
  • Skilled at handling situations in her own unconventional way

Manny Delgado

  • Manuel Alberto Javier Alejandro "Manny" Ramirez Delgado
  • Son of Gloria Pritchett and Javier Delgado
  • Stepson of Jay Pritchett
  • Half-brother of Joe Pritchett
  • Mature, intellectual, and romantic beyond his years
  • Enjoys poetry, literature, and the finer things in life
  • More sensitive and sophisticated compared to other kids
